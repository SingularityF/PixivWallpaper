FEATURES

Smart selection algorithm

The application implements a smart selection algorithm that selects the best illustration for your screen resolution. Save the trouble to browse through the illustrations, our algorithm will make sure you always get the best looking illustration for your desktop!
See our demo here.

Easy-to-use interface

A double-click is all you need to change your wallpaper, and your desktop will start to look amazing. Add the application to startup to let it change wallpaper for you every day, without human interaction.

Pick your personal favorite

You can pick your favorite wallpaper from our ranking gallery and have it delivered to your desktop!

Image supersampling

Don't worry if the illustration is too small for your screen, the application's built-in waifu2x support will make your wallpaper look smooth and clear.
